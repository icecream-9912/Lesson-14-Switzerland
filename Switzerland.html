Switzerland

What Is Switzerland

Switzerland is a country that lies in the heart of Europe. It shares its 1,935km-long border with five countries: Italy France, Germany, Austria and the Principality of Liechtenstein. Bern is the capital city of Switzerland. It is referred as 'federal city' by the Swiss.

Switzerland’s Greenery

In spring (March to May) the trees blossom and the meadows turn green. Sometimes in April the winter returns for a short period and sometimes there are summer conditions as early as May. In autumn (September to November), the fruit ripens and the leaves of deciduous trees change colour. Switzerland also has many mountain ranges, forests, gardens and more green nature.
